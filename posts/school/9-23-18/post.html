What's your pick of the week?

My pick of the week is really nerdy, but I hope you stay with me. I really love documentaries, I really love youtube, and I really love old computers. So together, here's all 3.

The 8-bit guy on youtube has a few interesting videos, but my favorite series he does (which is still on going) are on the Commodore computer line. If you don't know Commodore is one of the original personal computing companies, having released their first computers around the same time Apple first arrived on the scene. The 8-bit guy follows the history of this now defunct company by going through every piece of hardware in their aging lineup, from the original PET, presumably to the Amiga. I highly reccemond the series.

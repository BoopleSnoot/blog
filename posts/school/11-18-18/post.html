What are your experiences on Project 4?

Project 4 was a bit of work, but a lot of fun. I feel like I learned a lot, unfortunately those lessons were made in what not to do. I had a huge "bug" in the way my program was designed that caused Valgrind not to pass any tests. Essentially Valgrind would complain that I had unitialized memory being accessed. I eventually discovered the cause (after hour of trial and error). The root of the problem was I created and initialized lists of user-defined objects inside of another user defined class. I wasn't populating the lists first and then passing them as arguments to the constructor. This made Valgrind angry, because to the memory program, it had no way of checking inside the constructor that I actually initialized the lists in time to use them! Of course I remedy the situation by initializing anc constructing the lists, and then copying them on Construction of my main class.

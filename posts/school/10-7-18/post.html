If it were a true project I could go back and edit the codebase, but seeing as how its a school project I think I settle with learning from my mistakes.


What's your pick of the week?

Monads

So after the weekly reading "The Open-Closed Principle" I realized that I had run across this type of thinking while researching and doing Functional Programming. Monads are hard to wrap your mind around at first but I do believe they're trying to acheive a similar level of modularity of state that Object Oriented Programming tends to evangalize. If you've ever had to deal with Javascript or even the Java type-casting system, you've probably used the ideas behind monads without a formal structure. I highly recommend looking into the subject.

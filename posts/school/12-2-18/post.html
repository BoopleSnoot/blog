What are your experiences on Project 5?

Project 5 was fun! It was by far the smoothest project to develop for, and although it might have been the most "complicated" design, it felt like the fastest to program. I think this was for a number of reasons. One, by this time in the semester the workflow is basically second nature. Two, the experience from Project 4, a pretty similar project, felt easily translatable. Three, designing the program beforehand gave me a pretty good mental image of the code before I started even working.


What's your pick of the week?

Alpha Pilot

So I heard whisperings about this a while back, but essentially it's just starting to pick up some wind. Lockheed Martin is starting a competition for AI controlled/piloted drone systems. Your ultimate goal really is to get the drones to fly a drone race as fast as possible... autonomously. The real catch is your realistic competition are HUMAN piloted drones. Winning the competition against other bots is all well and good, but there is extra (monetary) incentive if even the human can be bested. I encourage you to click the link above if the contest sounds interesting and it feels like something worth competing in. Total cash awards are 2 million, so it's not like the work would go without reward.

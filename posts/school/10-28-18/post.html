What did you think of the Atlassian talk?

I thought it was very informative. I think it's interesting to see what other companies are like. I was really curious to hear about their working and lab environment at Atlassian. Since I've always been on more of an classical engineering team rather than one that is focused purely on developing software, I really wanted to know how their workflow and development cycle differed from my experiences. I got to find out a little bit about their habits after the speech.


What's your pick of the week?

OpenAI Blog

My pick of the week is OpenAI's DoTA project. I recently just started playing the game. It's a very nuanced, and a highly situational type of game that is difficult for humans to even master. For instance I've only been playing for 2 weeks and I can still barely help my team out. The fact that the team has created a program that can play a successful game, much less win is very shocking and highly important. I think it is a very important hallmark in history for computer science.
